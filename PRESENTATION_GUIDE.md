## ğŸš¨ Anticipated Questions & Answers

### Q: "Is the AI actually working?"

**A**: "For this MVP, we're simulating the AI logic with a sophisticated scoring algorithm that considers skill complementarity, industry alignment, and behavioral fit. The backend AI integration is our next step, but the frontend demonstrates the complete user experience."

### Q: "How do you ensure people actually complete the Academy?"

**A**: "Two mechanisms: 1) The $4 fee creates commitment. 2) You can't access full matchmaking features until you graduate. The platform literally gates the most valuable feature behind completion."

### Q: "What if people just skip through the tasks?"

**A**: "Great question. In the full version, tasks will require submission (e.g., upload your customer interview notes, submit your MVP link). Mentors will review and approve. For this demo, we show the interaction model."

### Q: "How is this different from LinkedIn?"

**A**: "LinkedIn connects anyone. We create qualified co-founders. Every person on our platform has:

- Completed a rigorous 12-week program
- Built a working MVP
- Created a pitch deck
- Been assessed for behavioral fit
  It's like comparing a random meetup to Y Combinator."

### Q: "Can this work outside Ethiopia?"

**A**: "Absolutely. The model is universal - any country with universities and an emerging startup ecosystem can benefit. We're starting with Ethiopia because we know the context, but this scales globally."

### Q: "What's your go-to-market strategy?"

**A**: "Phase 1: Partner with Jimma University as a pilot (500 students). Phase 2: Expand to other Ethiopian universities. Phase 3: Government and incubator partnerships. Phase 4: Regional expansion (Kenya, Rwanda, Ghana)."
